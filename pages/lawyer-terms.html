Professional standards and responsibilities for J.A.I legal practitioners

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Professional Legal Practice

These terms outline your professional responsibilities and benefits as a verified legal practitioner on the J.A.I platform.

1. Lawyer Eligibility and Verification

To practice on J.A.I, you must:

  • Active Bar License: Maintain a current, valid license to practice law in your jurisdiction
  • Professional Standing: Be in good standing with your local bar association
  • Verification Process: Complete our comprehensive verification including bar number, credentials, and background check
  • Continuing Education: Maintain required continuing legal education (CLE) credits
  • Professional Insurance: Carry appropriate malpractice insurance coverage

Professional Ethics Compliance

All lawyers must adhere to their jurisdiction's professional conduct rules and maintain the highest ethical standards in client representation.

2. Your Professional Responsibilities

  • Competent Representation: Provide competent legal services within your areas of expertise
  • Client Confidentiality: Maintain strict attorney-client privilege and confidentiality
  • Timely Communication: Respond to client inquiries within 24-48 hours
  • Professional Conduct: Adhere to all applicable professional conduct rules
  • Accurate Profile: Keep your profile, specializations, and credentials current
  • Quality Service: Provide professional, high-quality legal services
  • Fee Transparency: Clearly communicate fee structures and billing practices

3. Platform Benefits for Lawyers

J.A.I provides qualified lawyers with:

  • Client Matching: AI-powered matching with clients who need your expertise
  • Practice Management: Tools to manage cases, appointments, and client communications
  • Secure Platform: Encrypted communication and document sharing
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment collection and processing
  • Professional Profile: Showcase your expertise, experience, and credentials
  • Marketing Support: Increased visibility to potential clients
  • Administrative Tools: Streamlined case and client management

4. Fee Structure and Payments

For Lawyers:

  • Platform Fee: 15% commission on successful client engagements
  • No Upfront Costs: No membership fees or setup charges
  • Direct Fee Setting: You set your own hourly rates and fee structures
  • Secure Payments: All payments processed securely through the platform
  • Monthly Payouts: Regular payment processing and disbursement
  • Transparent Billing: Clear breakdown of platform fees and earnings

5. Professional Standards and Quality

  • Response Time: Maintain professional response times to client inquiries
  • Case Management: Use platform tools to keep clients informed of case progress
  • Professional Communication: Maintain professional tone and conduct in all interactions
  • Specialization Accuracy: Only accept cases within your areas of competence
  • Conflict Checking: Perform appropriate conflict of interest checks
  • Quality Metrics: Maintain satisfactory client ratings and feedback

6. Platform Policies and Compliance

  • Advertising Rules: Comply with legal advertising and solicitation rules
  • Data Protection: Follow all applicable privacy and data protection laws
  • Professional Liability: Maintain appropriate professional liability insurance
  • Regulatory Compliance: Adhere to all local, state, and federal legal practice regulations
  • Platform Guidelines: Follow J.A.I community guidelines and terms of service

7. Account Management and Termination

  • Profile Updates: Keep your professional information current and accurate
  • Availability Status: Manage your availability for new cases
  • Voluntary Termination: You may deactivate your account at any time
  • Ongoing Obligations: Existing client relationships continue beyond platform use
  • Professional Records: Maintain appropriate case records and documentation
