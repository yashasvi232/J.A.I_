Client Terms & Conditions

Your rights and responsibilities as a J.A.I client

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Important for Clients

These terms specifically outline your rights and responsibilities as a client using J.A.I to find and connect with legal professionals.

1. Client Services

As a J.A.I client, you have access to:

  • AI-Powered Lawyer Matching: Our intelligent system matches you with qualified attorneys based on your legal needs
  • Secure Communication: Protected messaging and document sharing with your chosen lawyers
  • Case Management: Track your legal matters and appointments through your dashboard
  • Transparent Pricing: Clear fee structures and no hidden costs
  • Quality Assurance: All lawyers are verified and maintain professional standing

2. Your Responsibilities as a Client

  • Accurate Information: Provide truthful and complete information about your legal needs
  • Timely Communication: Respond promptly to lawyer communications and requests
  • Fee Payment: Pay agreed legal fees and platform charges on time
  • Confidentiality: Maintain confidentiality of sensitive legal information
  • Respectful Conduct: Treat lawyers and platform staff with respect and professionalism

3. Platform Fees and Billing

For Clients:

  • Initial consultation fee: ₹299 (significantly lower than typical ₹2,000 consultation fees)
  • No platform membership fees for clients
  • Legal service fees are agreed directly between you and your chosen lawyer
  • All fees are clearly displayed before you commit to any service
  • Refund policy available for unused consultation credits

4. Your Rights as a Client

  • Choice of Lawyer: You can choose from multiple qualified lawyers for your case
  • Transparent Pricing: All costs are disclosed upfront with no hidden fees
  • Quality Service: Expect professional, competent legal representation
  • Privacy Protection: Your personal and case information is kept confidential
  • Support Access: 24/7 platform support for technical issues
  • Case Switching: Option to consult another lawyer if not satisfied with initial consultation

5. Privacy and Data Protection

Your privacy is our priority:

  • All communications are encrypted and secure
  • Personal information is never shared without your consent
  • Case details are only accessible to you and your chosen lawyers
  • You can request data deletion at any time
  • We comply with all applicable data protection laws

6. Limitation of Platform Liability

J.A.I serves as a connection platform:

  • We facilitate introductions but do not provide legal advice directly
  • Legal service quality is the responsibility of individual lawyers
  • Case outcomes depend on various factors beyond our control
  • Our AI recommendations are tools to assist, not replace, professional judgment
  • We maintain quality standards but cannot guarantee specific legal outcomes

7. Account Management

  • You can update your profile and preferences at any time
  • Account deactivation is available through your dashboard
  • Ongoing lawyer relationships continue even if you leave the platform
  • Case history and documents remain accessible for legal record-keeping
